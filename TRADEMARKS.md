## Usage Guidelines

### Permitted Use

You may use the AEGIS trademarks to:

- Refer to the AEGIS project and its components accurately
- Indicate compatibility with AEGIS specifications
- Discuss, review, or report on the AEGIS project

### Requirements

When using AEGIS trademarks:

- Use the trademark symbol on first use in any document or page
- Do not alter the marks (e.g., do not change capitalization or wording)
- Do not use the marks in a way that suggests endorsement by Finnoybu IP LLC without written permission
- Do not incorporate the marks into your own product names, service names, or domain names

### Not Permitted

You may not use the AEGIS trademarks to:

- Imply endorsement, sponsorship, or affiliation without explicit written permission
- Create confusion about the source or origin of products or services
- Register domain names, social media accounts, or similar identifiers that incorporate the marks

## Reporting Misuse

If you believe AEGIS trademarks are being misused, please open a trademark inquiry issue in the relevant repository or contact the project maintainers.

## Contact

For trademark licensing inquiries or permission requests, open a **Legal & Licensing** discussion in any AEGIS repository or contact the maintainers directly through GitHub.
